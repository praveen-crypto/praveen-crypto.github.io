VascularSim

Open-source Cardiovascular Simulator

VascularSim is an opensource software for cardio-vascular simulation developed by Touch Lab â€“ IIT Madras.

Simulate blood pressure, flow and volume in any part of the artery tree. Perform expirements and study their effects.

This simulator can be used by medical students and researches involving quantitative physiology.

Customize parameters

User set values for Blood and heart properties such as elastance, resistance and inductance.

Perform Experiments

Simulate multiple experiments and study effects. Models continiously added.

Analyze Waveforms

Analyze the waveforms and calculate pressure, flow, velocity and other values.

WHAT WE DO...

Simulate blood pressure and flow in all of the major arteries

Simulate physiological conditions like stenosis

Perform practical experiments
